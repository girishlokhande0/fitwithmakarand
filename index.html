Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ's

What types of fitness training do you offer?

-

I offer personalized training programs including weight loss, muscle gain, strength training, HIIT, and functional workouts — both in-person and online.

Do you offer online personal training sessions?

-

Yes, I provide live 1-on-1 online training via Zoom or Google Meet, along with customized workout plans and weekly progress check-ins.

Do I need any equipment for home workouts?

-

No equipment? No problem! I design routines that use your body weight or minimal tools like resistance bands and dumbbells if available.

Can you help with meal plans and diet guidance?

-

Absolutely! I provide personalized meal plans based on your fitness goals, dietary preferences, and lifestyle to help you stay on track.

How do I get started with your training program?

-

Just fill out the contact form or message me directly on WhatsApp. We'll schedule a free consultation to understand your goals and build a plan.

Do you train beginners or people with medical conditions?

-

Yes, I customize workout plans for all levels — including complete beginners. I also work with clients who have injuries, mobility limitations, or specific medical conditions after proper evaluation and consultation.

How long will it take to see results?

-

Results depend on your consistency, current fitness level, and goals. Most clients start noticing physical and mental improvements within 3 to 6 weeks of regular training.

What are your training session timings and availability?

-

I offer flexible training slots in the mornings and evenings on weekdays and weekends. Session slots are available on a first-come basis, so it's best to book in advance.
